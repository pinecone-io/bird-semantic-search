The Pirre warbler (Basileuterus ignotus) is a species of bird in the family Parulidae. It is found in the borderline area of Panama and Colombia (Serranía del Darién and Serranía del Baudó). Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist montane forests. It is threatened by habitat loss.
